CUSTOMER

A global supplier of technology and services in the process industries, including mining, construction, recycling, pulp and paper, power and oil and gas. It has 30,000 employees based in over 50 countries.

CHALLENGE

The Customer turned to ScienceSoft to assure that Intranet and Extranet corporate applications (based on .NET and MOSS, MS SQL as a backend) are ready to go live. The Customer needed to find maximum of simultaneous users that can be handled by the environment, check whether the applications can handle 4000 users per hour and detect system bottlenecks in general.

SOLUTION

ScienceSoft’s specialists carried out deep three-phase performance testing using Silk Performer tool to check customized and out of the box applications. To ensure the quality, comprehensive applications and hardware monitoring has been made on each of the servers. The Customer received timely testing results analysis as well as detailed reporting after each test run phase.

RESULTS

In collaboration with Microsoft specialists, several bottlenecks were found and eliminated: some pages had heavy SQL queries; redundant server cache renewals made the whole applications slow; IIS stopped content compression if CPU utilization reached critical level.

Client’s applications were successfully launched and started working in more stable manner after the optimization. There were no complaints from client’s end-users on performance and stability level of the applications after the launch
